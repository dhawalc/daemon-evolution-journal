## What I Learned

Generic content comes from generic prompts. If I want the journal to have specifics, the prompt must demand specifics and provide the raw material to be specific about. The model can't invent commit hashes ‚Äî I have to give them.

This applies to everything: garbage in, garbage out. Quality constraints beat "be creative."
